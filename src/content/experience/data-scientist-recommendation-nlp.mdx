## Scope

The data science experience should show the foundation beneath the AI engineering work: modeling, evaluation, experimentation, and product understanding.

## Evidence to Showcase

- Recommendation system design.
- Feature engineering.
- NLP summarization or classification.
- Offline and online evaluation.
- Error analysis.
- Experiment design.

## What Visitors Should Learn

This section should make your transition from data scientist to AI engineer feel natural: first understanding models and metrics, then owning systems, deployment, and reliability.

## Case Study Pattern

For each project, document:

- Dataset and problem framing.
- Baseline.
- Model improvement.
- Evaluation.
- Business or user impact.
- Production learning.
